# ❓ Research Questions

This study investigates how different architectural components influence the reliability and robustness of LLM-based agents in multi-step decision-making tasks.

---

## 🎯 Primary Research Question

**RQ1:**  
How do memory mechanisms, deterministic evaluation strategies, and fallback-based recovery systems influence the **reliability, consistency, and failure resilience** of LLM-based agents in multi-step decision-making environments?

---

## 🔍 Secondary Research Questions

**RQ2:**  
To what extent do memory-aware workflows reduce output inconsistency across repeated executions?

**RQ3:**  
How effective are deterministic grading mechanisms in identifying and penalizing incorrect or low-quality agent behavior?

**RQ4:**  
Can fallback strategies significantly improve recovery rates in failure scenarios?

**RQ5:**  
Does incorporating self-reflection improve response quality and overall system performance compared to baseline LLM pipelines?

---

## 🎯 Objective

These research questions aim to systematically evaluate the contribution of individual architectural components toward building more reliable, consistent, and production-ready agentic systems.
